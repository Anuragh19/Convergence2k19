    • About Us

    VNRVJIET (Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology) had been established by Vignana Jyothi in 1995 and has thus entered into its Silver Jubilee year in the Academic Year 2019��20. The Institute is sponsored by Vignana Jyothi, a non-profit organization founded in 1991 with an idea of imparting purposeful education to youth. The Vignana Jyothi Educational Society is guided by eminent and successful Industrialists, Entrepreneurs and Professionals who had won recognition for their professional competence and business ethics, with a common desire to serve the community by providing value-based education. The Society thus runs six other educational institutions in and around Hyderabad, spanning across the entire spectrum of primary and high school education, arts and sciences, management, polytechnic, engineering and technology, rural development and agriculture technologies. This year, the Institute celebrates the Silver Jubilee Year of its inception, supported by the able Management of Vignana Jyothi patrons, with the inspiring motto of Vignana Jyothi, i.e. "tamasoma jyotirgamaya" (progression, from darkness into the light) literally holding the beacon for its success.

    Convergence is the national-level, annual technical symposium of VNRVJIET. Since its inception in 1999, it has evolved as a great learning platform that complements the usual course work and brings forth lots of diverse passion and skills among the students. Each year's edition adds a feather in the cap of the Institute, with a wide spectrum of events and activities including but not limited to technical events, workshops, technical paper presentations, social activies, and more. Convergence 2k19 this year adds more life to the symposium by putting student innovations at the centre of it and aligns better with the changing arena of engineering where the conventional demarcations of different engineering disciplines are virtually absent.

    Convergence 2k19 introduces Hardware and Software Hackathons, Social Impact and Design Thinking Project Competitions, and Startup and Patent Idea Competitions, apart from the other usual events such as Technical Paper and Poster Presentations, Technical Workshops, other Technical Events, and activities of social responsibility to emphasise on sustainability and a necessary social dialogue.
